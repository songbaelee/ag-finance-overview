About This Resource

Ag Finance Overview started as an internal presentation. I was mapping out different potential approaches for an agri-SME lending facility, and realized that before getting into specific options, I needed to lay some general groundwork — how big the market actually is, what the main types of financing look like, and who supplies it. That groundwork kept growing, and eventually it made more sense as a standalone site than a slide deck.

The examples throughout draw heavily on Africa, since that's the market I know best — the general concepts should translate elsewhere, but the specifics (numbers, players, instruments) are most reliable in that context.

This is an independent project, not an official publication of any organization. If you spot something that's outdated, unclear, or worth adding, feedback is welcome.

Ag Finance Overview is a resource for development staff without a finance background.
